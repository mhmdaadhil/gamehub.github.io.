Top 5 Action Games of 2024

1.Devil May Cry 5 Devil May Cry 5 marks the return of the same wise-cracking Dante that we’ve come to love over the years, and this time, he has two playable buddies tagging along for the ride. Nero, who we first saw in Devil May Cry 4, has now lost his devil arm, but thanks to his new friend Nico, he can swap out different augmentations using his weaponized prosthetic arm called a Devil Breaker. 2.Bayonetta It took a while for Platinum Games’ first major success to arrive on PC, and even though parts two and three are Nintendo exclusives, the original Bayonetta is still one of the finest action games of all time. As the delicate balance between good and evil is in danger, you step into the stiletto-gun shoes of the Umbra Witch Bayonetta. And boy, she has creative ways of taking down the malicious angelic hosts. 3.Devil May Cry HD Collection Really? Three more Devil May Cry games? You’re absolutely right. In this collection, you receive two of the best action games of all time, and a bad sequel that we don’t really talk about… The HD collection is the perfect way to introduce yourself to the action game genre. If you’re an action game fan who wants to experience the classics, you won’t need to break out a PlayStation 2 to relive the good old days. 4.Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance Another Platinum Games hit here, and while it’s one of the oldest games on the list, Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance is still a bonafide classic. You play as the heavily augmented half-cyborg ninja Raiden, who is sent to confront a private military company intending to destabilize the fragile peace and plunge the world into another war on terror. 5.Nier Automata As one of the best PC games in 2017, it’s unsurprising that the chaotic Nier: Automata appears on this list. Platinum Games has a long history of making the best action games out there, and this collaboration with Square Enix is one of its more experimental efforts. Our Nier Automata review explains it as “an intelligent, bombastic game that’s in turns frenetic and tender, while deviating from cliche at every opportunity.”

How to Build a Gaming PC on a Budget

Yes, you can build a gaming PC within a Rs 30,000 budget. Look for components like an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processor, 8GB of RAM, and a dedicated graphics card such as NVIDIA GTX or AMD Radeon RX. Opting for a solid-state drive (SSD) will also improve boot and game load times.
